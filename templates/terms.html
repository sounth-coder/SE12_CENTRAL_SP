1. Overview

The Girra Student Portal (“Portal”) provides students with access to school-related information and features, including announcements, resources, and the CENTRAL Assistant (“CENTRAL”). By using the Portal, you agree to comply with these Terms, school ICT requirements, and any relevant Department policies.

Plain English: Use the portal for school stuff, be safe online, and follow school rules.

2. Eligibility & Accounts

  • You must be a current student (or authorised user) to access protected features.
  • You are responsible for activity on your account, including any actions taken using your login credentials.
  • You must keep your password private and use only your own account (no sharing).

If you believe your account has been accessed by someone else, you should change your password immediately and notify school staff.

3. Acceptable Use

You agree that you will not:

  • Attempt to bypass access restrictions, security controls, or page-level permissions.
  • Use the Portal to harass, threaten, defame, or impersonate others.
  • Upload, request, or share personal information (yours or someone else’s) beyond what is necessary for school use.
  • Use scripts, automation, or repeated requests to overload services (e.g., spamming CENTRAL).
  • Redistribute school-provided materials outside approved channels.

Misuse may result in access restrictions and/or referral to appropriate school processes.

4. CENTRAL Assistant

CENTRAL is designed to help with school-related questions (resources, navigation, assessment reminders, and general portal guidance). It may not always be correct, complete, or up-to-date.

  • Do not enter passwords, private contact details, or sensitive personal information.
  • CENTRAL responses should be verified against official school sources where required.
  • Use CENTRAL respectfully—no abuse, harassment, or inappropriate prompts.
Academic integrity: CENTRAL can support learning, but it must not be used to submit dishonest work or violate assessment rules.

5. Resources & Copyright

Resources provided through the Portal may include teacher-made content and third-party materials. You agree to:

  • Use resources for personal study and school purposes only.
  • Not repost, re-upload, or distribute materials publicly or to people outside authorised access.
  • Respect copyright and licensing requirements attached to specific files.

Some resources may be access-gated by year level for safety and curriculum reasons.

6. Privacy & Logging

To protect users and school resources, the Portal may log access and usage data for security and auditing. This can include timestamps, resource views, and technical metadata (such as IP address and browser user-agent).

  • Logs are used for security, abuse prevention, and service improvement.
  • Access to logs is restricted to authorised administrators/staff where appropriate.

If you have concerns about data handling, contact the school via the details in the Contact section.

7. Security

  • Do not attempt to exploit vulnerabilities, guess passwords, or access other users’ data.
  • Report bugs responsibly to school staff (do not share them publicly).
  • Keep your device secure (lock screen, updated browser, avoid public shared logins).

Security incidents may be investigated, and access may be suspended to protect the community.

8. Availability & Changes

The Portal is provided on an “as is” basis. Features may change, be updated, or temporarily unavailable for maintenance. Terms may be updated over time; continued use after updates indicates acceptance of the revised Terms.
