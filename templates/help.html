Frequently Asked Questions

  • • How do I reset my password?
    Use the 'Forgot Password' link on the login page to receive a reset link via your school email.
  • • Where can I find my timetable?
    Your timetable is available in the Resources section or through the Google Classroom link.
  • • How do I report a technical issue?
    Email support@girra.nsw.edu.au with details of the issue, including screenshots if possible.
  • • Can I access the portal from home?
    Yes, the portal is accessible from anywhere with an internet connection using your student credentials.
